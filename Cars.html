Lightning Mc Queen

Lightning McQueen is a rookie racecar in the Piston Cup series and secretly disdains his sponsor Rust-eze, hoping to be chosen by the more prestigious Dinoco team. Initially ungrateful,obnoxious, selfish, and sarcastic, Lightning believes that he doesn't need a crew chief or muchhelp from his pit crew to win races.

Hudson Hornet

The Hudson Hornet is a classic American car produced by the Hudson Motor Car Company from 1951 to 1954. It featured a unique "step-down" design, with a lower center of gravity for improved handling. The Hornet was powered by a powerful inline-six engine, which made it a dominant force in early NASCAR racing. It was available in various body styles, including sedans, coupes, and convertibles

Chick Hicks

Chick Hicks is a fictional character from the Disney-Pixar "Cars" franchise. He is portrayed as the main antagonist in the first "Cars" movie, where he is a competitive, unscrupulous race car determined to win at any cost. Chick is characterized by his green paint job, an abundance of sponsorship stickers, and his number 86. Known for his dirty racing tactics, he often crashes other cars to gain an advantage.

The King

The number 43 Dinoco car is a character from the Disney-Pixar "Cars" franchise, modeled after the famous NASCAR driver Richard Petty's car. Known as "The King," this blue race car sports the iconic Dinoco oil company logo and is voiced by Richard Petty himself. The King is a seasoned and respected veteran in the Piston Cup racing series. His character embodies sportsmanship and mentorship, especially towards Lightning McQueen.

Mater

Mater is a beloved character from the Disney-Pixar "Cars" franchise, known for his tow truck role and folksy charm. He has a rusty, old-fashioned appearance with a light blue and brown color scheme, and is voiced by Larry the Cable Guy. Mater is Lightning McQueen's best friend and provides comic relief with his humorous, carefree personality.Despite his clumsy demeanor, he often proves to be resourceful and brave.

Sally Carrera

Sally Carrera is a key character in the Disney-Pixar "Cars" franchise, portrayed as a sleek, blue 2002 Porsche 911 Carrera. Voiced by Bonnie Hunt, she serves as the town attorney of Radiator Springs and becomes Lightning McQueen's love interest. Sally is known for her intelligence, compassion, and dedication to revitalizing Radiator Springs. Her backstory reveals that she left a fast-paced life in Los Angeles for the charm

Winford Bradford

Winford Bradford Rutherford, commonly known as Winford, is a character from the Disney-Pixar "Cars" franchise. He is a race car competing in the Piston Cup series, sporting a number 64 and a maroon and white color scheme. Winford represents the RPM (Revolting Pistons Motor) sponsor, with his name and logo prominently displayed on his body.
